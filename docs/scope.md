# Project Scope

## In Scope
- Conversational recommendation system
- Periodic product catalog extraction
- CRM for conversational interactions
- Workflow-based orchestration

## Out of Scope
- Shopping cart management
- Product click tracking
- Payment and checkout
- Marketing automation
- User profiling across platforms

## Assumptions
- Product data is publicly available
- Users can be anonymous
- Recommendations are advisory, not transactional
